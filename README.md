Business Strategy Researcher & AI/ML Practitioner
-------------------------------------------------

I'm a business strategy researcher at Warwick Business School, investigating how leadership dynamics impact organizational performance. My research examines how CEO communication patterns and status influence strategic outcomes using Machine Learning and AI based robust research methods. My repositories showcase projects that apply computational approaches to business strategy questions, demonstrating how AI can provide deeper insights into leadership effectiveness and organizational performance. I'm especially interested in developing tools and methodologies that explain business phenomena rather than predicting outcomes.

* 🌍  I'm based in Coventry, United Kingdom
* ✉️  You can contact me at [shantanu.sharma@warwick.ac.uk](mailto:shantanu.sharma@warwick.ac.uk)
* 🧠  I'm learning Natural Language Processing
* 🤝  I'm open to collaborating on projects combining strategic decisions with AI/ML
